About Craft Your Career

Craft Your Career is the most-advanced website for online career assessment, career guidance and career counselling in India, designed for school students and graduates. Craft Your Career helps you discover your perfect career through its 5- dimensional career assessment, and revolutionary approach to career counselling and career guidance.
